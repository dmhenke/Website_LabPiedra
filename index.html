Our Research

🧬

Molecular Epidemiology

Investigation of RSV genetic variation and evolution across populations and disease contexts.

🛡️

Immune Correlates

Characterization of humoral and cellular immune responses determining viral clearance and disease severity.

📊

Viral Surveillance

Comprehensive monitoring of respiratory illness in children and febrile infants.

💊

Therapeutic Development

Preclinical and translational research on vaccines and antiviral compounds.

Advanced Research Models

The Piedra Lab has developed innovative pre-clinical models including human nose organoids (HNO) that recapitulate host-virus interactions for studying RSV and SARS-CoV-2 pathogenesis.
